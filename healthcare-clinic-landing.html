FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common concerns about visiting MedCare Plus.

How do I book an appointment at MedCare Plus?
+
You can book through our website form above, call us at +91 800 123 4567, or walk in to our reception. Online bookings are confirmed within 1 hour during business hours. For emergencies, please call directly or visit our 24/7 ER department.
What documents should I bring for my first visit?
+
Please bring a valid government ID (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport), any previous medical records or prescriptions, insurance card (if applicable), and a list of current medications. For children, bring their vaccination records and birth certificate.
Does MedCare Plus accept health insurance?
+
Yes, we are empaneled with 40+ insurance providers including Star Health, New India, ICICI Lombard, Max Bupa, and all government schemes including Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY). Our billing team will assist you with cashless processing at admission.
What are your working hours?
+
Our outpatient department operates Monday–Saturday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday: 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Our emergency department and pharmacy are open 24/7, 365 days a year. The laboratory opens at 7:00 AM daily for fasting sample collection.
Can I get my lab reports online?
+
Yes! All lab reports are available on our patient portal within 24–48 hours of sample collection. You'll receive an SMS notification with your login credentials. Critical results are communicated directly by phone to the patient and referring doctor.
Do you offer home visit or teleconsultation services?
+
We offer video/phone teleconsultations for follow-up appointments and non-emergency consultations. Home collection for lab tests is also available within a 15 km radius. Home visits by doctors are available for select elderly care and post-operative patients — contact us for details.
